This is a quiz. You answer some questions, and based on those questions, an algorithm calculates which of four cities your answers correspond to according to values defined to me based on my impression of those cities. Scientifically speaking, it's pure garbage. However, just like with astrology and quiz sections in dubious magazines, that doesn't mean it's useless. It might give you something to think about. You might inadvertently learn something about yourself. Not because I or my algorithm understand you but because perhaps YOU understand you but don't know it yet. That's really all I can dare to hope.

Have a great day, whoever you are!
